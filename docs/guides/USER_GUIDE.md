## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit the AstraLearn AI homepage
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right
3. Enter your email and create a secure password
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your learning profile

### Setting Up Your Profile

Your learning profile helps AstraLearn AI personalize your experience:

- **Learning Style**: Visual, Auditory, Kinesthetic, or Mixed
- **Reading Speed**: Set your comfortable reading pace (WPM)
- **Learning Goals**: What you want to achieve
- **Preferences**: Theme, font size, accessibility options

## Speed Reading

### Starting a Reading Session

1. **Choose Your Content**
   - Browse the content library
   - Upload your own text or documents
   - Use OCR to extract text from images

2. **Customize Your Settings**
   - Adjust reading speed (200-1000 WPM)
   - Set chunk size preference
   - Choose focus word highlighting style

3. **Begin Reading**
   - Click "Start Reading"
   - Text appears in intelligent chunks
   - Focus word is highlighted
   - Progress bar shows completion

### Reading Controls

#### Keyboard Shortcuts
- `Space`: Play/Pause
- `←`: Skip backward (10 chunks)
- `→`: Skip forward (10 chunks)
- `R`: Restart from beginning
- `+/-`: Increase/decrease speed

#### On-Screen Controls
- **Play/Pause Button**: Control reading flow
- **Speed Slider**: Adjust WPM in real-time
- **Quick Speed Presets**: 300, 500, 700 WPM buttons
- **Progress Bar**: Click to jump to any position

### Adaptive Speed Reading

AstraLearn AI automatically adjusts reading speed based on:

- **Content Complexity**: Technical content slows down

- **Reading History**: Learns your preferences over time

## Personalized Learning

### AI Tutor

Your personal AI tutor provides:

- **Socratic Questioning**: Thought-provoking questions
- **Concept Explanations**: Tailored to your level
- **Learning Paths**: Customized progression routes
- **Real-time Help**: Ask questions anytime

#### Asking Questions

1. Click the "Ask Tutor" button
2. Type your question
3. Receive personalized explanation
4. Get follow-up questions to deepen understanding

### Custom Learning Paths

1. **Set Your Goal**
   - Navigate to "Learning Paths"
   - Click "Create New Path"
   - Describe what you want to learn

2. **AI Generates Your Path**
   - Analyzes your current knowledge
   - Identifies knowledge gaps
   - Creates step-by-step plan
   - Suggests resources

3. **Follow Your Path**
   - Complete each step in order
   - Take assessments to verify understanding
   - Get recommendations for next steps

### Practice Exercises

After reading sessions:

- **Auto-generated Questions**: Based on what you read
- **Multiple Choice**: Quick comprehension checks
- **Fill in the Blank**: Test recall
- **Essay Questions**: Deep understanding
- **Instant Feedback**: Immediate scoring and explanations

## Tips & Best Practices

### Optimizing Speed Reading

1. **Start Slow**: Begin at comfortable pace
2. **Gradually Increase**: Add 50 WPM weekly
3. **Focus on Comprehension**: Speed without understanding is useless
4. **Use Focus Word**: Let your eyes follow the highlight
5. **Minimize Subvocalization**: Don't "speak" words in your head
6. **Take Breaks**: 5 minutes every 25 minutes

### Effective Learning

1. **Set Clear Goals**: Know what you want to achieve
2. **Space Your Sessions**: Distributed practice works better
3. **Test Yourself**: Regular self-assessment
4. **Apply Knowledge**: Use what you learn
5. **Teach Others**: Best way to solidify understanding
6. **Stay Consistent**: Daily habit beats cramming

### Using the AI Tutor

1. **Be Specific**: Clear questions get better answers
2. **Ask Follow-ups**: Dig deeper into concepts
3. **Request Examples**: Real-world applications help
4. **Challenge Yourself**: Ask "why" and "how"
5. **Review Explanations**: Revisit complex topics

### Maximizing Engagement

1. **Set Daily Goals**: Small, achievable targets
2. **Track Streaks**: Build momentum
3. **Join Challenges**: Community motivation
4. **Celebrate Wins**: Acknowledge progress
5. **Connect with Peers**: Social learning
6. **Review Progress**: Weekly reflection

### Content Selection

1. **Match Difficulty**: Choose appropriate level
2. **Follow Interests**: Learn what you love
3. **Mix Topics**: Varied content prevents burnout
4. **Progressive Difficulty**: Gradually increase complexity
5. **Return to Basics**: Review fundamentals
